salfet-palestine

The city of Salfit is located between the cities of Nablus and Ramallah, specifically to the southwest of the city of Nablus. It is built on a hill overlooking a spring of water (a large water basin), which was the source of water for the town and the surrounding area. Since Salfit is the center of the governorate, it was responsible for supervising the water spring in the entire region.
